Grammar legend
  • Noun: A person, place, thing, or concept. Sidewalk, umbrella, teacher, bathtub, nose, and love are all nouns.
  • Verb: An action word. Run, pitch, jump, and swim are all verbs.
  • Verb (-ing): Add -ing to a verb. Running, pitching, jumping, and swimming are all verbs with -ing added.
  • Adjective: Describes something or somebody. Lumpy, soft, ugly, messy, and short are all adjectives.
  • Adverb: An adverb tells how something is done. It usually modifies a verb and usually ends in -ly. Modestly, greedily, carefully, and very are all adverbs.
  • Length of time: Words like days, ages, centuries, and seconds are all lengths of time.
  • Mode of transportation: Words like car, truck, bicycle, and helicopter are all modes of transportation.
