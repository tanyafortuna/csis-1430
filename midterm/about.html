About Us

Every night I dreamt of pizza. Every morning I planned my day around when I would be eating pizza. Every afternoon I scoured the internet for pizza recipes and toppings ideas. Every night I ordered newer and better pizza stones and ovens.

One day I realized - I may as well open my own business so I can write all these things off as business expenses!

And that, my friends, is how Pippi's Pizza Parlor began.
