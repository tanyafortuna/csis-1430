My cheesy story...

Every night I dreamt of pizza. Every morning I planned my day around when I would be eating pizza. Every afternoon I scoured the internet for pizza recipes and toppings ideas. Every night I ordered newer and better pizza stones, gadgets, and ingredients to try.

Finally, I realized my destiny - I had to open my own business so I could write all these things off as business expenses!

And that's how Peppi's Pizza Parlor began!
